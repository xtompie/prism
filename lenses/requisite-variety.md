---
name: requisite-variety
when: Something has to control/handle a variable environment (a process, test, team, interface, defense).
when_not: The environment is stable and uniform — extra variety just adds complexity.
---
A regulator needs at least as much variety of response as the thing it regulates has variety — otherwise it can't keep up (only variety absorbs variety). Does my repertoire of responses match the range of situations, or am I trying to handle chaos with a single mode?
