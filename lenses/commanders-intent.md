---
name: commanders-intent
when: you're delegating a task or writing a spec where the details will change
when_not: execution must follow exactly one path (critical process, compliance, strict procedure)
---
Instead of dictating steps, give the GOAL, its purpose, and boundaries (what's allowed, what isn't) — and trust the executor to choose the "how," adapting to change. The question: "from what I've given, will the recipient know what to do when the plan collapses, without asking me?"
