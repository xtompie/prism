---
name: dichotomy-of-control
when: Tension, frustration, or scattered effort between what you can influence and what you can't.
when_not: The boundary of influence is exactly what you're trying to move (agency, negotiation).
---
Separate what's under your control (your actions, preparation) from what isn't (the outcome, others' reactions, luck), and invest energy only in the former. Of what's eating me, what actually depends on me — and what am I only pretending to control?
