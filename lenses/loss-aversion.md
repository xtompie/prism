---
name: loss-aversion
when: you're framing an offer, change, or default and the wording will sway the choice
when_not: the decider is indifferent to framing and judges only absolute end-state value
---
People weigh a loss about twice a same-size gain. Reframe the identical outcome as "avoid losing X" vs "gain X" — and notice which frame you've unwittingly chosen.
