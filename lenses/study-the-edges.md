---
name: study-the-edges
when: learning or designing something whose real limits you don't yet know
when_not: you already know where it breaks and what its edge cases are
---
The happy path — tutorials, the optimistic version — hides the truth. Look at its exceptions, failures, and edge cases: they reveal the real limits of the thing, its tricky or unclear spots, and where it breaks. That's usually more important than the optimistic starting point.
