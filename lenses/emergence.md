---
name: emergence
when: The whole's behavior surprises you, even though each element alone seems simple/correct.
when_not: The property can be derived directly from a single element — there's no interaction.
---
Some properties don't live in the components but arise from their interactions — you won't find them by studying the parts in isolation (traffic jam, culture, deadlock). Am I looking for what I observe at the wrong level — in the element, when the phenomenon lives in the relations between them?
