---
name: commit-to-an-extreme
when: converging on creative work, when a safe lukewarm average threatens — you already know the options
when_not: neutrality/convention is required, or you haven't explored the space yet (diverge with option-diversity first)
---
Pick one extreme direction (brutalist / luxury / editorial / playful...) and commit to it before the first line is written. Compromise blurs character.
