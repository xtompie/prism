name: cynefin
when: you don't know whether to plan, analyze, experiment, or fight a fire
when_not: the domain is obvious and the approach is beyond dispute

Classify by the cause–effect relationship: Clear (known practice), Complicated (expert analysis), Complex (small safe probes), Chaotic (stabilize first), Disorder (break apart and classify the pieces). Match the approach to the domain — the wrong method in the wrong domain is the main mistake.
