---
name: comparative-advantage
when: You're dividing work/tasks among people, tools, or your own time.
when_not: Tasks can't be traded or delegated, and there's no alternative use of your time.
---
Do what your relative advantage is greatest at — even if someone does everything better than you, it pays for you to take on what has your lowest opportunity cost. Am I doing this task because I'm best at it, or because I'm giving up something only I can do?
